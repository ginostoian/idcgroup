Business Values

International Debt Collection Group values the importance of long-established relationships, and our team handles cases with sensitivity, discretion and respect with the intent to recover not only monies, but also relationships.

The IDC Group team is trained in communication, and we believe that this is part of our success. Our team establishes meaningful connections in order to resolve disputes as quickly, and as peacefully, as possible. In situations where an immediate solution can’t be found, the team is well versed in legal negotiations and proceedings.

We’ll offer support every step of the way, including making necessary calls, notices and visits, and carrying out immigration and police checks.

Our Services

Business to Business debt can be crippling for companies of any sizes, it’s important to act decisively and quickly with any B2B debt. IDC Agents have particular experience and knowledge in recovering accounts in this sphere.
